Hotwire Demo

Welcome to the Hotwire Demo. You can find individual features on the subpages.

  • Simple Turbo-Frames: showcasing interaction with only a part of your page.
  • Turbo-Frames: Lazy loading and remote interaction. Showcasing how to lazy load parts of your page and interact with frames that are elsewhere on the page.
  • Turbo Frames: breaking out. Showcasing how to leave the environment of a Turbo Frame
  • Turbo Streams: Showcasing doing multiple updates at once.
  • Turbo Streams over WS: Sending turbo-streams updates over Websockets.
  • Turbo Streams over SSE: Sending turbo-streams updates over SSE.
  • Turbo Streams over WS with CE: Using a Custom Element to set up the TurboStreams WS Connection.
