Turbo Streams over Websockets

This page shows how to do Turbo Streams Updates over Websockets

It has several sections that will get updated over a Websocket connection that transports turbo-streams messages.

To enable that from the client we need to invode turbo.connectStreamSource() that registers a listener and handler.

The button below will do exactly that (using a Stimulus Controller) and also establishes a new WS Connection to the Backend to obtain the updates. We will then get an update every two seconds.
